A. LATAR BELAKANG

Produk Kreatif dan Kewirausahaan is one of the vocational subjects, for all skill programs for vocational students. This subject is taught for four semesters, namely in class XI and XII. The number of face-to-face hours for PKK subjects is an important competency that helps students to get into work or open a business independently.

B. DESKRIPSI

This module is a teaching material that discusses PKK subjects for class XII with the following scope of discussion:
1. Produksi Massal
2. Metode Perakitan Produk Barang/Jasa
3. Pengujian Produk
4. Promosi Produk
5. Strategi Pemasaran Produk
6. Evaluasi Perkembangan Usaha
7. Pengelolaan dan Laporan Keuangan Perusahaan
